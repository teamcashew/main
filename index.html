Our Manifesto: Forging Interactive Art

We are Team Cashew, a two-person, one-dog creative powerhouse rooted in the fertile soil of Brazil's Paraíba Valley. Our mission is to challenge the definition of a "game." We see pixels as paint and code as poetry, crafting interactive art that resonates deep within the soul. Our creed is "game by the game"—a solemn vow to pour every ounce of our passion into singular, uncompromised experiences. We are not a content factory; we are artisans building legends, one masterpiece at a time.

The Fellowship

The Architect, the Anchor, and the Guardian of our Sanity.

Júlia Klee

Creative Director & Lead Alchemist

The heart of our creative forge. With a spirit fueled by childhood wonder and hands fluent in the arcane language of code, Júlia weaves intricate worlds from pure imagination. Since conjuring her first game at age ten, she has been on a relentless quest to use technology as a canvas for emotion, building not just games, but gateways to other realities.

Melissa Melo

Producer & Supporting Director

The guiding star and the anchor in the storm. Melissa navigates the treacherous waters of indie development, transforming bold visions into concrete realities. With a keen eye for detail and a pragmatic heart, she provides the essential feedback, structure, and resources that keep the creative flame burning brightly, ensuring our epic quests reach their destination.

Marley

Chief Morale Officer & Goodest Boy

The silent, furry partner and the soul of Team Cashew. Marley's KPIs include tail wags per minute and successful nap sessions. He is the master of de-stress protocols (via belly rubs) and ensures optimal studio morale through rigorous, snack-based quality control. His presence is a constant, calming reminder of the simple joys that inspire our work.
